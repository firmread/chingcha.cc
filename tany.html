TANY Thai Artists in New York

Corporate Identity / Social Media Campaign / Web Development

Founded in 2015, TANY is a nonprofit organization collective with roots in New York's D.I.Y. culture dedicated to building platforms for diversity in Thai arts culture at every level from artists to audiences, connecting the independent Thai artists with the broader New York art community, identifying exciting new voices in Thai artists from around the world and providing them exposure to new audiences.

 

Reed serve as Chief Strategic Officer, Chief Technology Officer for TANY. Jan and Nitcha had contributed heavily in visual design works.
